How to make Honey Muffins.
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a small bowl.
  2. Mix eggs, honey, oat milk, and vanilla in a seperate larger bowl.
  3. Add the dry mixture to the liquid one in two batches, mixing thoroughly.
  4. Distribute mixture into greased muffin tins.
  5. Bake for 16 mins, or until lightly browned.
